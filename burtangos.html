Burtangos!

Description

In this recipe, we will find out how to make the perfect Burtango. If you love to spice up your meal, this is the one for you!

Ingrediants

  • 1 pound of chicken, cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 bell peppers, chopped
  • 1 jalapeno
  • 1 cup of white or brown rice
  • 1 avocoda, mashed into guacamole
  • 1 package of large wraps
  • sour and salsa as desired
  • shredded cheese of your choice

Steps

  1. In a frying pan, brown onions and bell peppers.
  2. Add chicken once onions and peppers are finished cooking.
  3. Meanwhile, make rice following suggested instructions for rice.
  4. Once chicken and rice are done, lay a large wrap on a plate and begin layering ingredients and toppings as desired.
  5. Bake in a preheated oven for a short while to make the wrap crispy and delicious.
  6. Enjoy!
