Brisket

Description

In this recipe, we will find out how to make the perfect Baked Ziti. If you love pasta and cheese, this is the one for you!

Ingrediants

  • 1 large brisket
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 pound of small potatoes
  • 1/2 pound of baby carrots
  • 1/2 pound of mushrooms
  • Any other vegetables if you desire
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, as desired

Steps

  1. In a large frying pan, brown the outside of the brisket.
  2. Once browned, add brisket to a large slow cooker or crockpot.
  3. Add all vegetables and garlic on top of the brisket.
  4. Fill the brisket with water and seasoning. You can also add beef or chicken stock as desired.
  5. Let cook for 6-10 hours or until meat is tender and falling apart.
  6. Enjoy!
